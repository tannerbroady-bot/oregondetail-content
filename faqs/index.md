## How much does a full detail cost?

Packages start at $280 for the Deluxe Detail and go up to $625 for the Ultimate Detail. Final price depends on vehicle size (S/M/L) and condition. View full pricing at oregondetail.com/service-menu.

## Do I drop off my car, or do you come to me?

All services are performed at our shop at 860 Conger St Unit 14, Eugene, OR 97402. We do not offer mobile detailing. This is a deliberate choice — our indoor climate-controlled facility delivers better, more consistent results than any mobile setup.

## How long does a detail take?

Most details take 3–8 hours depending on the package and vehicle condition. Ceramic coating jobs require the vehicle overnight at minimum. Multi-stage paint corrections may require multiple days. We recommend arranging a ride for most services.

## My car stinks — what service will fix that?

Odor removal starts with a Complete Interior Detail, followed by a combination of Enzyme Treatment, Ozone Treatment ($75+), and Cabin Air Filter replacement ($40) depending on severity. For mold or biohazard situations, call us directly at (541) 968-2804.

## What's the difference between wax and ceramic coating?

Wax sits on top of paint and lasts 6–8 months. Ceramic coating chemically bonds to the clear coat, creating a semi-permanent barrier measured in years — not months — with deeper gloss, hydrophobic water beading, and stronger protection. Ceramic coating starts at $750 for a 2-year package.

## My headlights are yellow and foggy — can they be fixed?

Yes. Headlight restoration starts at $50 per headlight. Includes wet sanding, machine polishing, and application of a UV ceramic coating with a 2-year warranty.
