# Auto Detailing & Ceramic Coating — Eugene, Oregon

Oregon Detail Co. is Eugene, Oregon's trusted destination for professional auto detailing, ceramic coatings, paint protection film (PPF), and paint correction. Located at 860 Conger St Unit 14, our expert team serves Eugene, Springfield, and the greater Lane County area with premium detailing packages for every budget — from a quick interior refresh to a full concours-level paint restoration.

Whether you're looking to deep clean your daily driver, protect your new car with a long-lasting ceramic coating, remove stubborn odors and pet hair, or restore your classic car to show-winning condition, Oregon Detail Co. delivers meticulous results backed by years of experience.

We also specialize in RV detailing, headlight restoration, and mold remediation. Every appointment is handled with care in our dedicated Eugene shop — a certified Carfax service center. We ensure your vehicle gets the focused attention it deserves.
