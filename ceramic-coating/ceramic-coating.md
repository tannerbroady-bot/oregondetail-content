## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does ceramic coating last?**
It depends on the package. Level 1 carries a 2-year warranty. Level 2 and Level 3 carry 5-year warranties and include five years of complimentary annual exterior maintenance. All packages are backed by an in-house warranty maintained by the same team that installed them.

**Does ceramic coating prevent rock chips and scratches?**
Ceramic coating increases resistance to light swirls and environmental damage, but does not protect against rock chips or deep scratches. For physical impact protection, paint protection film (PPF) is the correct product. Many vehicles benefit from PPF on high-impact zones combined with ceramic coating over the entire vehicle.

**What does the installation process look like?**
Every installation follows the same process: full hand wash, clay bar paint decontamination, enhancement polish, then panel-by-panel coating application followed by indoor cure. The entire process takes at least a full day plus 12 hours of indoor curing time minimum.

**Is ceramic coating worth it for Oregon drivers?**
Eugene and Springfield's combination of heavy tree canopy dropping sap and pollen, wet winters causing water spotting, and UV-heavy summers makes ceramic coating a strong investment. For most Willamette Valley drivers who plan to keep their vehicle 3+ years, the math works strongly in favor of at least a Level 1 installation.

**How do I maintain a ceramic-coated vehicle?**
Regular hand washing with pH-neutral soap, avoiding automatic car washes with brushes, and attending your annual maintenance visit at Oregon Detail Co. Annual maintenance visits are included in all packages and keep the coating performing at its best for the full warranty period.

**Can I get ceramic coating on my RV, boat, or classic car?**
Yes. Oregon Detail Co installs ceramic coatings on RVs, boats, motorcycles, and classic cars. RV ceramic coating is particularly valuable in Oregon given the UV exposure and wet winters that accelerate oxidation on large painted surfaces. Call (541) 968-2804 to discuss your specific vehicle.
