# GlassParency Glass Protection — Eugene, Oregon

## What is GlassParency?

GlassParency is a professionally applied chemical treatment that enhances driver visibility in inclement weather by causing water to micro-bead and repel off your windshield. Night glare is also dramatically reduced, making your driving experience safer and more comfortable.

GlassParency is a two-part chemical application engineered only to react with glass (silica). Unlike waxes or topical treatments, GlassParency is a long-term solution that bonds directly to glass.

Every GlassParency automotive application is backed by a 3-year product warranty.

## Why GlassParency?

- One-time professional application
- No cure time — drive away immediately, even in rain
- 3-year product warranty
- Dramatically improved wet-weather visibility
- Reduced night glare and halos
- Repels water, bugs, and road grime from glass surfaces
- Engineered specifically for automotive glass

## Oregon Weather & GlassParency

Eugene and Springfield drivers deal with rain from October through June. GlassParency is particularly valuable in the Pacific Northwest — water sheets off the windshield at highway speeds, reducing the need for wipers and improving visibility in Oregon's heavy rain seasons.
